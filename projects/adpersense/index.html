AdPerSense - HackIllinois 2018

At HackIllinois 2018, my friend and I came up with AdPerSense. The objective of AdPerSense was to determine the success rate of print advertisements over different demographics. Because we were working with a limited time, we were only able to predict for the demographic of 18-24 year old computer science students. For that demographic we were able to reach 90% accuracy with our machine learning algorithm in 4 hours. Given a larger amount of survey data and more time to train the model, we would have been able to reach much higher success rates for this demographic.
